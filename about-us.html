About Us

Welcome to WellTech Care

WellTech care is one of the most trusted medical laboratories conducting PME services that offer accurate and detailed results by our professional doctors and deliver your results promptly.​

We ensure every patient is healthy, safe, and comfortable for the PME service one medical appointment at a time.
Diagnosis and Treatment Conducting diagnosis and treatment at a professional level every time a patient consults our PME services.
High Quality Laboratory Utilizing only top-notch laboratory equipment guarantees that all medical information will be delivered with the highest level of accuracy and processed promptly with little to no delays.

Our Mission

The team aims to allow patient appointments online and open up more avenues for patients to be diagnosed for required medical examinations. The project is dedicated to all users to offer a convenient way for faster and more efficient medical examination queueing.​

Our  Vision

The team aims to be a reliable and trusted online medium for patients and doctors to interact with the physical medical examination (PME) procedures. The team hopes to open more possibilities for patients and doctors to deepen their relationship to improve worldwide healthcare.
